# Pricing Review Proposals

These proposals do not create formal prices.

## Proposal PRICE-PROP-001

Keep candidate price evidence separated from production pricing authority.

Candidate evidence may include observed prices, candidate prices, price ranges, export manifests, or validator outputs. None of these may become a formal `PricingRule` without the owning MethodSpec / pricing review path.

Boundary: no formal price, no customer quote, no `BudgetEstimateLine`, no payment path.
