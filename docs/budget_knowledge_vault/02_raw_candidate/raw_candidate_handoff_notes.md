# Raw Candidate Handoff Notes

## Handoff Role

Raw Candidate hands candidate-only evidence to downstream review. It does not hand formal prices to the Budget Engine.

## Accepted Handoff Signals

- Candidate source evidence.
- Review queue evidence.
- Static guard evidence.
- Final packet or explicit Integration Officer acceptance.

## Non-authority Signals

- Candidate price values.
- Raw observed unit prices.
- Unreviewed material hints.
- Any AI-generated price explanation.

These signals must remain evidence, not production pricing rules.
