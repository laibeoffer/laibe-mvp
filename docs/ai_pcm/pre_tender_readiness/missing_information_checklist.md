# Missing Information Checklist

This checklist records data gaps before tender. Questions are routed to AI PCM 總監／後台總控 Agent, not directly to the user.

| Missing Area | Question to Resolve | Severity | Route |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic project identity | What is the project name, site, owner-side contact route, and target completion date? | blocker if absent | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Scope zones | Which rooms, floors, facades, systems, or work zones are included? | blocker if absent | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Drawings | Are dimensions, drawing version, affected boundaries, and fixed equipment shown? | blocker if absent | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Photos | Do photos cover all affected zones, access path, ceiling / wall / floor condition, utilities, and hidden-risk areas? | warning or blocker | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Budget | Is the budget range known, and are trade-level items and contingency separated? | warning or blocker | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Materials | Are material grades, finish, color, brand level, performance requirements, and substitutions defined? | warning or blocker | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Work method | Who handles demolition, protection, hauling, temporary works, testing, cleanup, and restoration? | blocker if unclear | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Exclusions | Which items are not included in the contractor price? | blocker if hidden | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Payment | What are payment milestones, acceptance evidence, retainage, and payment release criteria? | needs human review if disputed | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Acceptance | What objective criteria determine each milestone acceptance? | blocker if absent | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Site constraints | What are work hours, access limits, noise limits, elevator rules, security rules, and neighbor constraints? | warning or blocker | AI PCM Supervisor |
| Tender instructions | What submission format, bid deadline, Q&A process, and comparison basis are used? | blocker before launch | AI PCM Supervisor |

## Classification

- `blocker`: Cannot issue a fair or comparable tender without this information.
- `warning`: Tender may proceed only if the gap is disclosed and tracked.
- `clarification`: Useful detail that improves bid quality but does not block tender readiness.
- `needs_human_review`: Requires supervisor, legal, procurement, safety, or tender-system decision.
