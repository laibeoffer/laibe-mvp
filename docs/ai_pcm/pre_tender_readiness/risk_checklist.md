# Risk Checklist

| Risk | Trigger | Impact | Mitigation | Status Effect |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Incomplete scope | Missing zones, tasks, or responsibilities | Change orders, disputes, bid variance | Build a scope matrix and exclusion list | NOT_READY_FOR_TENDER |
| Insufficient drawings | Missing dimensions or unclear affected boundaries | Contractors price assumptions differently | Attach measured plan / SVG with revision date | NOT_READY_FOR_TENDER |
| Poor photo coverage | Photos omit hidden or high-cost areas | Site-condition claims after award | Add location-tagged photos and site notes | READY_WITH_WARNINGS or NOT_READY_FOR_TENDER |
| Budget omissions | Major trade, disposal, protection, temporary works, or testing omitted | Underfunded tender package | Add budget line items and contingency | READY_WITH_WARNINGS or NOT_READY_FOR_TENDER |
| Undefined materials | Grade, brand level, performance, finish, or substitution rule unclear | Non-comparable bids | Add material schedule and approved alternatives | NOT_READY_FOR_TENDER |
| Work-method ambiguity | Demolition, protection, sequencing, testing, cleanup, or restoration not assigned | Disputes and schedule delay | Add responsibility matrix | NOT_READY_FOR_TENDER |
| Hidden exclusions | Owner-supplied or excluded work not disclosed | Bid challenge and post-award conflict | Publish exclusion list in tender attachments | NOT_READY_FOR_TENDER |
| Payment mismatch | Payment milestones do not match measurable acceptance | Cashflow dispute | Tie payment to objective acceptance evidence | NEEDS_HUMAN_REVIEW |
| Legal / contract ambiguity | Formal contract text would need interpretation or amendment | Unauthorized legal change risk | Escalate to AI PCM Supervisor | NEEDS_HUMAN_REVIEW |
| Production system dependency | Tender launch, bidder access, DB, AI API, payment, escrow, listing fee needed | Out-of-scope system operation | Escalate; do not connect | NEEDS_HUMAN_REVIEW |
