# Likely Contractor Questions

Prepare answers and attachments for questions contractors are likely to ask before pricing.

## Scope

- Which rooms, elevations, systems, and work packages are included?
- What work is explicitly excluded?
- Are protection, cleanup, hauling, temporary works, and restoration included?
- Are there owner-supplied materials or owner-side vendors?

## Drawings And Site Conditions

- Are dimensions confirmed or for reference only?
- Which drawing / SVG revision should be priced?
- Are there photos for every affected area?
- Are there hidden utilities, ceiling constraints, waterproofing, structural, or access risks?

## Materials

- What material grade, finish, color, brand level, and performance criteria apply?
- Are substitutions allowed?
- Who approves samples, mockups, or alternatives?

## Budget And Pricing

- Is the bid lump sum, unit price, schedule of rates, or mixed?
- Which costs should be separately priced?
- How should optional items, alternates, or exclusions be submitted?

## Schedule And Work Rules

- What are work hours, access windows, elevator rules, noise restrictions, neighbor rules, and security requirements?
- Are site visits required or optional?
- What are the expected start date, duration, and milestone dates?

## Payment And Acceptance

- What evidence is required for milestone acceptance?
- Are retainage, warranty, defect correction, or final acceptance rules included?
- Who signs acceptance and what happens if acceptance is disputed?

## Tender Process

- What is the Q&A route and deadline?
- What is the required submission format?
- How will bids be compared?
- Are attachments part of the pricing basis?
