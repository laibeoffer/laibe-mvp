# Tender Readiness Checklist

Use this checklist before any formal tender launch. Each item should have evidence, a status, and an owner route.

| Area | Required Check | Evidence Expected | Blocker Condition |
|---|---|---|---|
| 需求表單 | Project purpose, scope, room / zone list, priorities, constraints, target date are filled | Approved demand form or case brief | Missing scope or contradictory requirements |
| 平面圖 / SVG | Dimensions, openings, fixed equipment, paths, affected zones, and drawing version are clear | Plan, SVG, marked-up drawing, or measured sketch | Missing dimensions for priced work |
| 現況照片 | Photos cover every affected area and key hidden-risk points | Photo set with location notes | No visual basis for demolition, protection, access, or installation pricing |
| 預算項目 | Budget includes major work packages, owner-supplied items, contingency, and exclusions | Budget sheet or estimate summary | Major trade or cost category absent |
| 材料規格 | Material grade, finish, color, brand level, performance and substitutions are defined | Material schedule or spec sheet | Material choice materially changes cost and is undefined |
| 工法範圍 | Demolition, protection, disposal, installation, testing, restoration, cleanup are assigned | Scope matrix or work-method note | Responsibility gap between owner and contractor |
| 不包含項目 | Exclusions and owner responsibilities are explicit | Exclusion list | Hidden exclusion would make bids incomparable |
| 付款 / 驗收節點 | Milestones, acceptance criteria, retainage, evidence and payment triggers are defined | Payment and acceptance schedule | Payment is detached from measurable acceptance |
| 招標文件 | Attachments, Q&A process, site visit rules, submission format, and bid comparison basis are ready | Tender package draft | Contractors cannot submit comparable bids |

## Minimum Status Rules

- Any blocker item makes the case `NOT_READY_FOR_TENDER`.
- Any legal, safety, procurement, system, or contract ambiguity that cannot be resolved by documents makes the case `NEEDS_HUMAN_REVIEW`.
- All required checks complete with minor tracked warnings may be `READY_WITH_WARNINGS`.
- All required checks complete with no unresolved blockers or unreviewed high-risk items may be `READY_FOR_TENDER`.
